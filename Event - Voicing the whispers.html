Voicing The Whisper

06 March 2022

"Voicing the Whisper" represented a pivotal moment in our collective mission to break the cycle of child abuse. By shedding light on this sensitive issue, we aimed to empower not only the children at Saranalaya Home but also the broader society. Our volunteers worked tirelessly to create a nurturing space where open discussions and learning flourished. Through interactive games, activities, and informative sessions, we aimed to foster resilience and equip the children with the tools to recognize and report any signs of abuse. By amplifying their voices and advocating for their rights, we aspired to create a community that stands together in protecting the innocence and well-being of every child. This event, although reaching a limited number of children, served as a catalyst for change, reminding us of the profound impact education and awareness can have on breaking the silence surrounding child abuse.
