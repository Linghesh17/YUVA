Yuvaneta'24

3rd October 2024

The investiture ceremony of Yi Yuva REC, YUVANETA'24, unfolded on October 3, 2024, with a sense of purpose and excitement. The event began with a prayer song that set a solemn tone, followed by a warm welcome from the emcees. Nodal officers Dr. Prem Kumar and Dr. Hemavathy addressed the gathering, speaking with heartfelt conviction and sparking a shared sense of inspiration among all present. Building on this momentum, Yi Chennai Co - Chair Mr. Varan Mittal energized the audience with his vision, while Regional Mentor Mr. Pratheep Chandramohan followed, reflecting on milestones that paved the way for future achievements. Their words encouraged every member to keep striving toward positive change. Past YUVA Chair Richard V shared reflections on his journey, speaking not only of accomplishments but of friendships and lessons that shaped the club. He presented the annual report, underscoring the club's progress, before introducing the new YUVA Chair, Linghesh B. The Council of 2024 - 2025 was installed by Linghesh B symbolizing a new generation of leaders. The YUVA Co - chair, Nandhini R, was then introduced and took the stage to install the Board of 2024 - 2025. Linghesh B went on to share an ambitious yet purpose - driven year plan, outlining the vision and goals for the months ahead. The Yi Chennai Chair, Mr. Tobin Jose, then spoke on the club's impact on society, reminding members of the meaningful difference they are capable of creating. In a closing gesture, Nandhini R conveyed words of thoughtful gratitude, her thanks resonating warmly with everyone present. The ceremony concluded on a reverent note with the National Anthem, leaving the gathering with a renewed sense of community and a shared promise to create positive change.
