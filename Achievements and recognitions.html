Future 4.0 Advocacy Documents 2024 - Health

2024

The members of Yi Yuva REC played a pivotal role in crafting the "Future 4.0 National Advocacy Documents" as a synopsis of the Health Vertical, which aimed at driving impactful change in society. These documents were presented to the special invitees and esteemed Yi dignitaries on 14th September 2024. The dignitaries included Dr. Dheepak Selvaraj, Associate Director of CMC Vellore; Dr. Devamani Pandian, HOD of Reproductive Medicine and Laparoscopic Surgery at the Narayani Group; Thiagarajan T, Joint Chair of the Yuva National Team; Vishal Mehta, Regional Chair of SRTN; Nitin Bothra, Chair of the Road Safety National Team; Mr. Naiju, Director of VIT Vellore; Gomathi Srikalyan, Regional Mentor for Health in SRTN; and Dinesh Agarwal, Regional Mentor for Road Safety in SRTN. With the exceptional guidance of Mr. Pradeep Chandramohan, Regional Mentor of Yi Yuva SRTN, Abraham Samuel, Rishi Karthick and Thrisha M collaborated closely to design the document. Their combined efforts resulted in a well-crafted and impactful presentation, showcasing a seamless blend of creativity and dedication. We sincerely thank Yi Chennai for offering us this remarkable opportunity to contribute to such meaningful initiatives and for their steadfast support throughout this journey.

Yi Chennai Annual Report 2023

2023

Our Yuva team had the honor of designing and drafting the Yi Chennai Annual Report for 2023. The report documented the achievements of the Yi Chennai Chapter through their projects and initiatives. It also highlighted the impactful accomplishments of the verticals within the Yi Chennai Chapter. This includes Yi Chennai Chapter's 34 Lakh digital reach, alongside affirming YUVA in 21+ Colleges and THALIR in 20+ Schools. On December 7th, 2023, our team had the privilege of attending the Yi Annual Day, where we presented the annual report in front of Mr. Dilip Krishna - National Chairman, Mr. Thiagarajan Thirunavukkarasu - Regional Chair (SRTN Region), and other CII delegates, Mr. Pratheep Chandramohan Chair-Yi Chennai Chapter, Mr. Srivas Anantharama- Co-Chair-Yi Chennai Chapter, Mr. Milan Wahi the upcoming vice chairman-CII Chennai zone, and the Vice Chairman Mr. Unni Krishnan the upcoming Chairman of CII Chennai zone. The Chair, Co-Chair, and designers of our club were presented with Yuva Inspiration Awards for designing the annual report. We are humbly proud of our achievements, which were only made possible through our teamwork and commitment. We look forward to continuing to contribute to the Yi community in any way we can.

Innovation Tool Kit

2022

We are thrilled to proudly present our latest accomplishment, the collaborative development and design of the groundbreaking book titled Innovation Tools for Practical Learning. This extraordinary achievement is the result of the dedicated efforts of our exceptional team members: Aalia, Richard and Sundar. The book boasts a comprehensive collection of 16 meticulously crafted activities, specifically tailored to cater to individuals aged 6 to 60. With a primary focus on fostering innovation and skill evaluation, it promises to be a transformative resource for personal and professional growth. We were honored to launch this remarkable book at the esteemed YES SUMMIT 2022, where it was inaugurated by the distinguished Educational Minister of Tamil Nadu - Mr. Anbil Mahesh. It emphasis on hands-on exploration, critical thinking, and problem-solving ensures an immersive learning experience across diverse subjects. Delve into its pages, embark on a rewarding journey, and let the power of practical learning inspire you!
