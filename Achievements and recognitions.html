The Future 3.0

2023

The delegates of YUVA REC was thrilled to have been an integral part of designing and drafting the document - The Future 3.0, where our team not only participated but also had the distinct honor of joining forces in Delhi for the inauguration. The Future 3.0 establishes its presence within the Y20 conclave, where young individuals from 46 chapters come together to brainstorm solutions for challenges within five key sectors: The Evolution of Technology in Manufacturing, Agricultural Technology, Education, Sustainable Lifestyle, and the Digital Economy and further be made into one single national document which under evaluation will be presented to the office of the Prime Minister of India as the nation’s biggest youth document. This visionary document, representing a collaborative effort by youth at a national level, serves as a testament to the remarkable outcomes that emerge when diverse perspectives unite under a shared objective. We were privileged to be in the audience of Smt. Meeta Rajivlochan, the Secretary of the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, who articulated the role of youth in driving India towards greatness.

Why Yi

2022

The book Why Yi explains what Yi does. It enlightens us about the youth membership in school, colleges and also about the projects and various other initiatives of Young Indians. The book was eulogized by the immediate past Yi National Chair Ms. Smita Vaish Agarwal, Chairman Chennai Zone CII Mr. Murugavel Janakiraman, Southern Region Tamilnadu Chair Yi Mr. Kalyana Sundaram. Five immensely skilled designers of the book “Why Yi" are from the YUVA club of REC. The designers are Sundar N, Richard V, Harini O K, Aalia Thabasum K S and Pritika K. The designers were crowned with the award “Creative Minds of the Year” for their work on this book and various other supports for Young Indians. The officials honored the students for their praise worthy efforts and dedication towards the goal. This event turned out to be a magnificent and spectacular one for the YUVA club of REC. We wholeheartedly thank Yi Chennai for the privilege of having us, it sure did motivate us to achieve more and work for the betterment of the society.

Innovation Tool Kit

2022

We are thrilled to proudly present our latest accomplishment, the collaborative development and design of the groundbreaking book titled Innovation Tools for Practical Learning. This extraordinary achievement is the result of the dedicated efforts of our exceptional team members: Aalia, Richard and Sundar. The book boasts a comprehensive collection of 16 meticulously crafted activities, specifically tailored to cater to individuals aged 6 to 60. With a primary focus on fostering innovation and skill evaluation, it promises to be a transformative resource for personal and professional growth. We were honored to launch this remarkable book at the esteemed YES SUMMIT 2022, where it was inaugurated by the distinguished Educational Minister of Tamil Nadu - Mr. Anbil Mahesh. It emphasis on hands-on exploration, critical thinking, and problem-solving ensures an immersive learning experience across diverse subjects. Delve into its pages, embark on a rewarding journey, and let the power of practical learning inspire you!
