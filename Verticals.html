Accessibility

Chair - Joshna James
Co Chair - Charanya R

Yi's Accessibility vertical was established in 2019 with a steadfast commitment to creating inclusive spaces and promoting societal awareness. Our focused efforts revolve around raising awareness on accessible solutions, thereby enhancing employability and improving the quality of life for differently abled individuals. Through a range of initiatives, including enlightening awareness sessions, the installation of access ramps, and the incorporation of Braille signboards, we strive to transcend physical barriers and cultivate a deep understanding of inclusion within society. Yi's noble pursuit infuses the landscape of accessibility with an aura of grace and dignity, propelling us towards a more enlightened and equitable society. Together, let us champion the empowerment of the differently abled, sowing the seeds of a brighter future where every individual is valued and included.

Arts And Culture

Chair - Pritika K

The Arts and Culture vertical plays a crucial role in preserving and fostering cultural heritage for the next generation. Practices of the olden times are recognised as a source of identity and pride, and strives to ensure its continuity and relevance in contemporary society. Through various initiatives like festive celebrations, dance performances, workshops, games and interactive sessions, we aim to engage the community and expose them to different art forms, traditions and practices. By providing platforms for traditional artists and craftsmen to showcase their talent, we create opportunities for cross-generational learning and exchange. Our efforts are rooted in the belief that a strong valued foundation is essential for the holistic development of individuals. The Arts and Culture vertical is dedicated to nurture appreciation, understanding, and preservation of our rich cultural heritage and carry it forward with pride to the future.

Climate Change

Chair - Gunasekar K
Co Chair - Linghesh B

The Sustainable Development vertical is dedicated to promote environmental consciousness among people and preserve our surroundings through strategic initiatives. We focus on various aspects of sustainability, with a particular emphasis on tree plantations and afforestation. Through our collective efforts, we strive to create a greener and more sustainable future for generations to come, where the delicate balance between human activities and the natural environment is maintained. Because you know a small act contributes to a better future!

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Chair - Nivedhitha G K
Co Chair- Jeyanth V

Young Indians (Yi) recognizes, entrepreneurship as a key element of transformation for India heading into the next decade of growth. This would not only create value in terms of economic wealth, but also be a trigger for job creation and is a path that is needed for solving the myriad of challenges that prevent India from reaching its potential. Innovation is a solution to a problem or a need which was unaddressed before - could be an Idea /method or a product . The ultimate objective may vary from commercializing or monetizing the product / service or could be a social innovation for larger good or just an innovation to explore the possibilities and extend the human evolution.

Health

Chair - Roshini K S
Co Chair - Ramya Kumari M

COVID-19 has underscored the importance of health and the prevailing disparities, urging Yi, a young leaders' organization, to prioritize this critical aspect in our role as Nation Builders. Within the health vertical, promoting better health and well-being for children and young people is paramount to Yi's Vision of Nation Building. Leveraging its CII Parentage and stakeholder focus, Yi employs a blend of Awareness Building and Implementable Campaigns. The ultimate goal is to achieve a Healthy India by 2030, addressing the 'ABC' of health and wellness through a comprehensive approach. Yi recognizes that health encompasses more than physical well-being, embracing compassion, empathy, and collective responsibility. Let us embrace this noble endeavor, uniting our passion and resolve as Yi becomes a guiding light towards a healthier, more vibrant India.

Masoom

Chair - Varsheni S
Co Chair - Thrisha

Yi Masoom embraces elegance as it passionately pursues its objective of spreading awareness about Child Sexual Abuse. Through workshops for primary school teachers, we equip them with essential knowledge and tools to address this sensitive issue effectively. Our engaging sessions educate children about safe and unsafe touch, fostering a sense of personal safety and dignity. Sensitizing parents is another crucial aspect of our mission, as we create a supportive environment that encourages open dialogue and prioritizes child protection. Through impactful rallies, campaigns, and community engagement, we raise public awareness and rally communities to safeguard our future generations. Additionally, our sessions on digital safety and captivating skits at schools ensure responsible navigation of the digital realm.

Road Safety

Chair - Mogesh Kanna S
Co Chair - Swathi G

The importance of safety on road, in a country like India cannot be overlooked by any organization or individual. One significant initiative in this regard is the Good Samaritan law, which provides legal protection to bystanders who come to the aid and rescue of road accident victims. This law encourages people to assist in emergency situations without the fear of legal consequences, thus promoting a culture of helping others Additionally, the Rural Road Safety Animal Protection program addresses the issue of stray animals on roads. It aims to protect these animals by providing them with reflective markers, enhancing their visibility and reducing the risk of accidents.In summary, these initiatives collectively contribute to enhancing road safety in India.

Rural Initiatives

Chair - Dharani J S
Co Chair - Kavvya J E

Rural Initiatives (RI) is an esteemed facet of the Yi stakeholders team, dedicated to engaging rural communities within Yi chapters. By building bridges to rural India, Yi fosters collaboration among the rural sector, local communities, and industry. Through impactful projects, Yi has the potential to make a significant difference in rural communities. Imbued with elegance, RI embodies Yi's unwavering commitment to uplifting rural landscapes. With transformative initiatives, Yi aims to create an environment that empowers and inspires rural citizens. Together, Yi and RI strive to effect lasting change in the lives of rural communities.
