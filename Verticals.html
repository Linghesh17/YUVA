Accessibility

Chair - Charanya R
Co Chair - Dharshan Nikesh R

Yi's Accessibility vertical was established in 2019 with a steadfast commitment to creating inclusive spaces and promoting societal awareness. Our focused efforts revolve around raising awareness on accessible solutions, thereby enhancing employability and improving the quality of life for differently abled individuals. Through a range of initiatives, including enlightening awareness sessions, the installation of access ramps, and the incorporation of Braille signboards, we strive to transcend physical barriers and cultivate a deep understanding of inclusion within society. Yi's noble pursuit infuses the landscape of accessibility with an aura of grace and dignity, propelling us towards a more enlightened and equitable society. Together, let us champion the empowerment of the differently abled, sowing the seeds of a brighter future where every individual is valued and included.

Health

Chair - Rithika Babu
Co Chair - Vishnu Venkateswaran

COVID-19 has underscored the importance of health and the prevailing disparities, urging Yi, a young leaders' organization, to prioritize this critical aspect in our role as Nation Builders. Masoom

Chair - Thrisha M
Co Chair - Adhya Shetty

Yi Masoom embraces elegance as it passionately pursues its objective of spreading awareness about Child Sexual Abuse. Through workshops for primary school teachers, we equip them with essential knowledge and tools to address this sensitive issue effectively. Our engaging sessions educate children about safe and unsafe touch, fostering a sense of personal safety and dignity. Sensitizing parents is another crucial aspect of our mission, as we create a supportive environment that encourages open dialogue and prioritizes child protection. Through impactful rallies, campaigns, and community engagement, we raise public awareness and rally communities to safeguard our future generations. Additionally, our sessions on digital safety and captivating skits at schools ensure responsible navigation of the digital realm.

Road Safety

Chair - Dharani J S
Co Chair - Abraham Samuel E

The importance of safety on road, in a country like India cannot be overlooked by any organization or individual. One significant initiative in this regard is the Good Samaritan law, which provides legal protection to bystanders who come to the aid and rescue of road accident victims. This law encourages people to assist in emergency situations without the fear of legal consequences, thus promoting a culture of helping others Additionally, the Rural Road Safety Animal Protection program addresses the issue of stray animals on roads. It aims to protect these animals by providing them with reflective markers, enhancing their visibility and reducing the risk of accidents.In summary, these initiatives collectively contribute to enhancing road safety in India.
