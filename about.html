"We Can! We Will!"

We, the YUVA club of REC, aspire to create a platform for young professionals and entrepreneurs to engage in nation-building activities. We focus primarily on providing the youth and fellow people with opportunities for

  • Networking
  • Leadership
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Social Responsibility

Yi Chennai

Yi Chennai refers to the Young Indians (Yi) chapter in Chennai, India. Young Indians is an integral part of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) and aims to create a platform for young professionals and entrepreneurs to engage in nation-building activities. Yi Chennai focuses specifically on the youth in Chennai, providing them with opportunities for networking, learning, and making a positive impact on society. The chapter organizes various events, workshops, and initiatives to foster leadership, entrepreneurship, and social responsibility among its members. Through its activities, Yi Chennai aims to empower young individuals, enhance their skills, and contribute to the overall development of the city and its youth community.

CII Chennai

CII Chennai refers to the Confederation of Indian Industry's Chennai Zone. The Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) is a premier business association in India that works to create an enabling environment for industry and facilitate economic growth in the country.CII Chennai organizes various initiatives, conferences, seminars, and events to address industry-related challenges, promote investment opportunities, enhance competitiveness, and encourage sustainable and inclusive growth in the region.
