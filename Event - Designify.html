Designi'fy

17th May 2024

Yi Yuva REC conducted Designi'fy, an intensive hands-on workshop focused on design fundamentals using Photoshop, at Rajalakshmi Engineering College. The workshop, led by Sundar N, attracted a diverse group of participants eager to enhance their design skills. He guided attendees through the intricacies of Photoshop, covering everything from importing images to mastering the wide array of tools the software offers. The enthusiasm and dedication of the participants filled the room, as they engaged deeply with the material and demonstrated a keen interest in expanding their design capabilities. Overall Designi'fy was a new experience and inspired participants to further explore the creative possibilities within Photoshop. The event not only enhanced participant's technical abilities but also sparked a greater appreciation for the art of design, making it a memorable and impactful session for all involved.
