Yi Chennai Annual Day 2023

7 December 2023

The Yi Chennai Annual Day took place at Grand Chennai by GRT Hotels, T. Nagar on the 7th of December 2023. Annually, this celebration is conducted to commemorate the achievements of the club and deliberate on forthcoming projects.The event was graced by the presence of the Yi and CII officials such as Mr.Dilip Krishna - National Chairman, Mr.Thiagarajan Thirunavukkarasu -Regional Chair (SRTN Region) and other CII delegates, Mr Pratheep Chandramohan Chair-Yi Chennai Chapter, Mr Srivas Anantharama Co-Chair-Yi Chennai Chapter, Mr Milan Wahi the upcoming vice chairman-CII Chennai zone and the Vice Chairman Mr Unni Krishnan the upcoming Chairman of CII Chennai zone. The Chair, Co Chair, and the Executive Council of the upcoming year were introduced. The highlight of the event was our college being honoured with two accolades, "The Maximum Number Of Events" and "Innovative Event". Richard-Chair , YUVA REC, Siva Santosh - Co-Chair, YUVA REC ,Thrisha, and our design team were awarded with the YUVA Inspiration Awards. YUVA REC was honoured with the responsibility of crafting the Annual Report of 2023. The aforementioned individuals, in collaboration with our editorial team, dedicated two months of concerted effort to produce the annual report which earned the acknowledgement it merited. The officials expressed their commendation for the students' exceptional and meritorious accomplishments.
