The Future 3.0 Youth Conclave

07 August 2023

The Future 3.0 Youth Conclave, in collaboration with Y20, is an event aimed at engaging young students. Organized by SKPC College, the event took place on August 7th at 9am. It's goal is to produce a document that will be presented to the G20 summit for review by our Prime Minister. This document, created by the youth of our country, aims to address various challenges our nation faces. On the following Youth Conclave Day, participants who were divided into groups such as agriculture and education previously, where asked to move to different rooms. They collaborated with other members of their team and brainstormed innovative solutions to these issues.
